MISSION

SIL operates across five pivotal sectors—Space Defense and Security, Space Exploration, Space Domain Awareness, Space Habitation, and Counterspace—each committed to essential initiatives sponsored by government entities. Embedded within these sectors, SIL's eight mission areas provide innovative solutions to the nation's most complex space challenges. 

IMPACT

SIL consistently delivers disruptive technologies and cutting-edge solutions that position it well ahead of industry standards, offering both strategic and technological superiority to government agencies and sponsors. This leadership in innovation ensures that SIL remains at the forefront of the space sector, pioneering novel capabilities and setting new benchmarks.

EXPERTISE

SIL is led by pioneering executives, spanning former senior government officials, award-winning DARPA researchers, and Cornell University faculty. Expertise extends across the entire spectrum of the space industry including both civil and defense applications. SIL employees bring a wealth of technical knowledge to tackle our sponsor's most pressing initiatives.

About

SIL is a key participant in DARPA's BRIDGES initiative, aimed at advancing space superiority through rapid integration of commercial innovations into defense systems. As a University Affiliated Research Center (UARC) for Cornell University, SIL leverages academic expertise to drive technological breakthroughs in national security space.
