MISSION AREAS & CAPABILITIES 

Information Mobility

Designs and develops agile and reliable collection and transportation of data in support of space mobility and logistics, space battle management, and C4ISR; Exploits developments in distributed computing to facilitate zero trust service exchange across assets; Engineers disruptive out-of-band communication concepts for spacecraft operating in electromagnetically denied environments; Develops sensor data fusion, normalization, and characterization technologies; Designs disruptive deep-space communication capabilities.

Space System Architectures and Mechanisms

Designs and develops next generation space-system architectures, incorporating innovations in electromechanical devices, distributed-spacecraft sensing modalities, use of in-space resources for ISAM-driven design, and payloads of widely varying length scale: 0.01 - 1000 meters in characteristic length. Develops scalable structures, deployment strategies and in-space manufacturing and assembly concepts to enable large aperture space systems on orbit, planetary capabilities infrastructure, next-generation planetary communication systems and remote sensing, IoT and SCADA systems, MEMS, planetary life support systems, planetary transportation systems, lunar and cislunar habitation platforms, deep space human settlement, space sustainability capabilities, planetary transportation systems, space traffic management systems, space situational awareness systems, planetary defense capabilities and in-situ resource utilization.

Space Security

Exploits developments in physics and computing to engineer space security, resilience, and protection technologies that increase the resilience of space assets and inform policy and military strategy; Encompasses all counter-counterspace measures including counter-ASAT, counter-DEW, counter-NAVWAR, counter-RPO, cyber defense, ECCM, and capabilities for the defense against unintentional threats such as structural health management systems, evasive maneuvers, and debris mitigation systems. Design and implementation of systems for protection against solar wind, high-energy particles, and disruptive space weather, especially critical in high-altitude and near-polar orbits; ionospheric modeling; and advanced thermal management systems.

verification and validation

Program and concept feasibility evaluation using agile development techniques and model-based systems engineering; Rapid proof-of-concept development for hardware and software; Conducts verification and validation throughout the systems engineering lifecycle including feasibility studies, concept of operations, system requirements, high-level design, detailed design, software/hardware development, unit testing, subsystem verification, system verification \& deployment, system validation, operations and maintenance, and changes and upgrades, particularly for TRL raising activities; and Rapid development of analytical frameworks for novel programs, services, and concepts.

space combat power projection

Designs and develops next-generation space combat power projection capabilities and counterspace systems that exercise space superiority in support of operations in all domains and neutralize or reduce the effectiveness of adversary actions. Exploits advancements in cyberwarfare, directed energy, plasma control, autonomy, electronic warfare, spacecraft dynamics, navigation warfare, computer science, information science, model-based systems engineering, robotics, and physics.

assured autonomy 

Exploits developments in machine learning, robotics, and autonomy to advance capabilities for space mission assurance, spacecraft dynamics, spacecraft characterization, satellite swarms, and rendezvous and proximity operations. Includes next-generation OSAM, ISAM, and on-orbit sustainment systems that leverage advancements in robotics, multi-agent machine learning, computer vision, and dynamics to service a spacecraft physically and remotely including spacecraft maintenance, reconstitution, residual operations, and disposal.

materials for space applications

Designs and develops materials tailored for extreme radiation and temperature environments that leverages state-of-the-art 3D printing technologies, such as powder bed fusion and powder flow-directed energy deposition; Microstructural analysis and mechanical characterization conducted on printed structures to establish relationships between processes, microstructures, and properties; Engineered living materials that couple conventional engineering materials with living organisms to confer dynamic life-like properties; Genetically engineer organisms to tailor biological properties and confer extremophile properties; Additive manufacturing of metal alloys and biological scaffolds using techniques developed for extreme space environments; Create materials databases and ontological knowledge graphs of space-based manufacturing processes and materials to enable novel approaches for space materials design; Advanced multiscale modeling and simulation techniques that are coupled to machine-learned models to enable digital twins of space processes; and Constitutive modeling of materials to capture their behavior over a wide range of environmental conditions and histories.

spacecraft propulsion and thermal management systems 

Designs and develops next-generation space combat power projection capabilities and counterspace systems that exercise space superiority in support of operations in all domains and neutralize or reduce the effectiveness of adversary actions. Exploits advancements in cyberwarfare, directed energy, plasma control, autonomy, electronic warfare, spacecraft dynamics, navigation warfare, computer science, information science, model-based systems engineering, robotics, and physics.

spacecraft guidance, navigation, and control

Designs and develops next generation space-vehicle navigation solutions, exploiting optical navigation techniques, signals of opportunity, multibody robotic actuation (including CMGs and reactionless motions of gripper actuators); Designs and implements technologies that leverage advances in momentum control systems, robotics, additive manufacturing, and high-performance computing; Addresses high-priority needs of space-domain awareness systems, in-orbit assembly and repair, and cislunar supply-chain logistics enabling new constructs in in-orbit assembly and test, reconfiguration, and modular space-system architectures.
